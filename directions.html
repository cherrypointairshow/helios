Directions to MCAS Cherry Point/Havelock, NC:

From South Carolina & Points South: Take Interstate 95 North to Highway 70 East. Follow Highway 70 East to Havelock, NC.

From Tennessee & Points West: Take Interstate 40 East to Highway 70 East. Follow Highway 70 East to Havelock.

From Virginia & Points North: Take Interstate 95 South to 70 East. Follow Highway 70 East to Havelock.

From Tennessee & Points West: Take Interstate 40 East to Highway 70 East. Follow Highway 70 East to Havelock.

Once you enter Havelock, get in the left lane and make a left on to Hwy 101 (Fontana Blvd.). The very next left is MCAS Cherry Point. Follow the signs and watch for Military Police guidance.

Where is the air show located?

The 2014 Air Show will be held aboard the East Coast's premiere Marine Corps Air Station, Cherry Point, NC 28533.

MCAS Cherry Point is centrally located in Havelock, NC. The air show is a short drive from anywhere in eastern NC.

Come visit us for a day of free family fun.
