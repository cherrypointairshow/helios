GEICO Military Tribute Bike

Recognizing Our Troops With Style

Designed as a tribute to U.S. armed forces and military personnel the GEICO Military Tribute Bike will be on display at the MCAS Cherry Point 2014 Air Show. The 2014 model of the bike was designed by Paul Teutul, Jr., from the hit television show "American Chopper." The GEICO bike displays shiny chrome accented by dark red and blue and a front wheel in the shape of a gold star.

The bike also features five medallions representing the five branches of U.S. military with the words "sacrifice, loyalty and honor" displayed.
