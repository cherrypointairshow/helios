Premium Seating

Admittance and parking are FREE. Four premium seating options are available...

Afterburner Chalet: Feel the awesome power of America’s premiere flying machines. The Afterburner chalet gets you closest to the action! It’s on the flight line and at show center.

This chalet features both outside and shaded seating. Your ticket price includes a meal and unlimited beverages. Meals are served from 6 - 8pm on Friday, 11am - 2pm on Saturday and Sunday. Beverages are available throughout the duration of the show.

General Seating Chalet: The General Seating Chalet puts you right on the flight line, where the energy is intense and your heart won’t stop racing. This chalet has both outside and shaded seating. Your ticket includes various snacks and unlimited beverages throughout the duration of the show.

Box Seats: Experience all the liveliness and excitement of the flight line with exclusive box seats. Bring your friends and family to share your personal box! This outside seating option is available in bundles of 4, 6 or 10.

Bleacher Seats: Get a great perspective! The bleacher seats are nestled against the box seating area and will give you a commanding, unobstructed view of the show. Sold as single seats.

*Tickets are only valid for the date purchased.*
