Media Accrediation

This form is required for any media interested in attending the air show, especially media interested in participating in our media rides program.

The Air Show is currently scheduled for May 16-18, 2014. The night show is scheduled for May 16 from 5:45-10 p.m. Day shows are scheduled for May 17 and 18 from 10:30 a.m. – 4:30 p.m.

Media day and media rides are currently scheduled for May 15. Pilots and air show leadership will be available during this period. Select media members will have the opportunity to ride performing aircraft.
