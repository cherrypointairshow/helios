Premium Seating

Admittance and parking are FREE. Four premium seating options are available for purchase.

Afterburner Chalet

The Afterburner chalet gets you closest to the action! It’s on the flight line and at show center. This chalet features both covered and uncovered seating. Your ticket price includes a meal and unlimited beverages. Meals are served from 6 - 8 pm on Friday, 11 am - 2 pm on Saturday and Sunday. Beverages are available throughout the duration of the show.

General Seating Chalet

The General Seating Chalet puts you right on the flight line, where the energy is intense and your heart won't stop racing. This chalet has both covered and uncovered seating. Your ticket includes various snacks and unlimited beverages throughout the duration of the show.

Box Seats

Experience all the liveliness and excitement of the flight line with exclusive box seats. Bring your friends and family to share your personal box! This outside seating option is available in bundles of 4, 6 or 10.

Bleacher Seats

Get a great perspective! The bleacher seats are nestled against the box seating area and will give you a commanding, unobstructed view of the show. Sold as single seats.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION

Tickets are only valid for the date purchased

Ticket prices are for ages 3 and up. No Refunds or Exchanges.

You must check in at the MAIN TICKET tent when you arrive at the show. You will receive a pass for entry to the appropriate seating area.

Parking is FREE.
