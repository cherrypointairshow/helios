Special needs

The air show experience

Because of the relative ease of getting around on our flat, cement tarmac, the Cherry Point Air Show serves a great venue for entertaining our special needs groups.

If you're planning on attending any of the three open to the public air shows, here are some helpful hints to make your air show experience exciting and easier:

Arrive early

A seating area for guests with special needs will be provided on the flight line. This seating area will inevitably fill up fast. Because of this, we ask that only (1) person accompany the special needs guest.

State-issued handicap parking priveleges

If you have a state-issued handicap parking privelege, please ensure it's displayed on the front of your vehicle and enter the air show through the main gate on highway 101.

Follow the signs designated for Special Needs, Handicapped and Disabled parking area. There will be transportation provided to those in wheelchairs or those with special needs to exclusive seating areas.

There will be transportation from the special needs parking area to the handicap seating area.

Be prepared

Security will be tight and all bags will be checked. If you have special medications, please NOTIFY the military police officer upon entry. Also note that you will be on an open, flat, cement, tarmac for the entire day. Bring sunscreen, hats, sunglasses and ear plugs.
