Volunteer Opportunities

Currently accepting volunteers

Fill the online form, or the pdf if your browser doesn't load the form, to become a part of the air show team.

PDF forms can be submitted via e-mail to AJ See or faxed to (252) 466-3287.

The deadline to sign up is May 5th. Volunteers under the age of 16 must have a chaperone 18 years or older.

For questions call, AJ See, Operations Directorate, Bldg. 1 (252) 466-5147

Social media, our contact page, or (252) 466-4241.
